Data Stream Format For The Omni Entertainment System

Data Streams Are Encoded as FSK

2 Pulldown Clicks: Enable Reader
1 Pullup Click: Start Reading
1 Pulldown Click: Stop & Process
2 Pullup Clicks: Disable Reader

Baud & Freq Is Automaticly detected
Initial Pattern 100001100001101010101010100 is used to determine baud and frequencies
